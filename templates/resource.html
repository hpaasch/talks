Remember the basics:

  1. Decide you want something.
  2. Try to figure out what exists.
  3. Check to see if anyone else has it or part of it.
  4. Ask in writing, nicely and clearly. CC someone who also cares or has standing.
  5. Wait. Check back. Wait. Check back. Communicate in writing. Keep a log.
  6. Appeal or escalate.
