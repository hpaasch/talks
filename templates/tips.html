Tips

  • FOIA the FOIAs. FOIA the fields. FOIA the talking points. FOIA the database layout.
  • Find the official right person, and also the unofficial contact who actually knows. Talk to someone. Make a verbal request. Surface the concerns. Inform your request.
  • Check to see if it is already available.
  • Always cite the statute.
  • If you believe a certain record exists, describe it.
  • Be reasonable about the time period.
  • Don't be a jerk. Follow the rules.
  • Be specific, not overly broad
  • Follow up.
